About Claud Spring

More Than a Company —
A Mission

Claud Spring is a Nigerian multi-sector firm dedicated to empowering communities through solar energy, sustainable agriculture, quality real estate, and innovative infrastructure.

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between sustainable living and practical business growth, we operate across Kwara and Oyo States, delivering end-to-end solutions that are affordable, reliable, and built to last.

Whether you're a homeowner looking to cut electricity bills, an investor seeking fertile farmland, or a business ready for a new building, our expert teams are here every step of the way.
