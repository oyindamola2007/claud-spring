Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a quote for solar installation? +

Simply fill out the contact form above or call/WhatsApp us on 09120935011. Our solar team will schedule a free site assessment and provide a detailed quote within 48 hours.

Do you handle documentation for farmland purchases? +

Yes. We handle complete title verification, surveying, deed of assignment, and registration processes. Every land transaction comes with full legal documentation and transparent pricing.

What areas do you currently serve? +

We primarily serve clients in Kwara State (Ilorin and environs) and Oyo State (Ibadan and environs). However, we have also completed projects for clients in other states — contact us to discuss your location.

How long does a typical construction project take? +

Timelines vary by project scope. A standard residential build typically takes 3–6 months, while renovations can range from weeks to a few months. We provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

Do you offer after-installation support? +

Absolutely. All our solar installations come with a maintenance plan and warranty period. Our biodigester systems also include follow-up service calls to ensure proper operation and performance.
